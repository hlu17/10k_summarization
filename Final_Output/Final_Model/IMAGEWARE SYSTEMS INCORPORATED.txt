Business Overview: The company is a pioneer and leader in biometric identification and authentication software, providing secure credentials, authentication security software, digital identification wristbands and badges, multi-factor authentication services, and law enforcement solutions. Its patented IWS Biometric Engine® is hardware agnostic and can utilize different types of biometric algorithms, enabling developers and system integrators to implement biometric solutions or integrate biometric capabilities into existing applications. In March 2020, a global pandemic was declared due to the novel strain of coronavirus (COVID-19). This has impacted the economic conditions in the US and worldwide, with the full extent of the impact unknown. The US government has responded with the Coronavirus Aid, Relief and Economic Security Act (CARES Act), although the company is unable to determine the impact it will have on its financial condition, results of operations or liquidity.

Revenues: Revenues from software and royalty increased 78% to $383,000 in 2020 compared to 2019, due to higher identification project related revenue of $539,000, offset by lower law enforcement project related revenue of $11,000, lower royalty revenue of $116,000 and lower sales of boxed identity management software sold through our distribution channel of $29,000. Revenue from the sale of hardware and consumables decreased $12,000 during the year ended December 31, 2020 compared to the corresponding period in 2019. Services revenue increased $937,000 during the year ended December 31, 2020 compared to the corresponding period in 2019 due to an increase in the service element of project related work completed. Cost of software and royalty product revenue increased approximately $18,000 due to the significant percentage of the revenue increase containing solutions with minimal third-party software costs. Cost of product revenue for our hardware and consumable sales decreased approximately $14,000 due to lower hardware and consumable product revenue. Cost of services revenue increased approximately $578,000 due to higher service revenue. Cost of maintenance revenue increased approximately $23,000 despite lower maintenance revenue of $29,000. We have focused on strategically updating our products with the latest mobile and cloud technology, relaunched imageware authenticate in July 2020, and plan to continue to enhance our identity platform products in 2021. Management believes that these initiatives will result in the expansion of our solutions into both law enforcement and non-governmental sectors.

Gross Profit Margin: Software and royalty gross profit increased 81% or approximately $365,000 for the year ended December 31, 2020 compared to the corresponding period in 2019, due to higher software and royalty revenue of $383,000 and minimal increase in software and royalty cost of revenue. Hardware and consumables gross profit increased $2,000 due to lower hardware and consumable revenue of $12,000 combined with lower cost of hardware and consumable revenue of $14,000. Services gross profit increased $359,000 due to higher service revenue of $937,000 combined with higher service cost of revenue of $578,000. Maintenance gross profit decreased 2% or $52,000 due to lower maintenance revenue of $29,000 combined with higher cost of maintenance revenue of $23,000.

Operating Income: For the year ended December 31, 2020, net cash used in operating activities was $8,009,000, consisting of a net loss of $7,253,000 and an increase in working capital and other assets and liabilities of $438,000, offset by non-cash income of $1,194,000. For the year ended December 31, 2019, net cash used in operating activities was $11,267,000, consisting of a net loss of $11,581,000 and an increase in working capital and other assets and liabilities of $287,000, offset by non-cash costs of $723,000 and non-cash income of $696,000.
