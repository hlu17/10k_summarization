Business Overview: We allocate the purchase price of acquired companies to tangible and intangible assets and liabilities based on their estimated fair values. This process involves significant estimates and assumptions, such as future expected cash flows, customer attrition rates, trademarks and discount rates. We also estimate the fair value of deferred revenue obligations using a cost build-up approach. These estimates are subject to uncertainty and may be affected by unanticipated events.

Revenues: publishing and advertising our publishing and advertising segment addresses market opportunities ranging from the diverse authoring and publishing needs of technical and business publishing to our legacy type and oem printing businesses. it also includes our platforms for advertising cloud, web conferencing, document and forms, and primetime. revenue from our publishing and advertising segment was $297 million in fiscal 2020, up from $263 million in fiscal 2019, representing 13% year-over-year growth. Extractive summary: Revenue from contracts with customers is recognized ratably over the term of agreements with customers, beginning with commencement of service. Subscription revenue is comprised primarily of fees charged for subscription and hosted service offerings, and related support. Product revenue is comprised primarily of fees related to licenses for on-premise software. Services and other revenue is comprised primarily of fees related to consulting, training, maintenance and support, and advertising offerings. In the Digital Media segment, Creative Cloud and Document Cloud revenue increased due to increased demand and digital engagement amid the work-from-home environment. Digital Experience revenue increased primarily due to subscription revenue growth across the offerings. Publishing and Advertising revenue increased due to the Advertising Cloud segment move. Annualized Recurring Revenue (ARR) in the Digital Media segment grew to $10.18 billion at the end of fiscal 2020, up from $8.33 billion at the end of fiscal 2019. Digital Experience revenue was $3.13 billion in fiscal 2020, up from $2.80 billion in fiscal 2019, representing 12% year-over-year growth. Publishing and Advertising revenue was $297 million in fiscal 2020, up from $263 million in fiscal 2019, representing 13% year-over-year growth.

Gross Profit Margin: % Gross Profit Margin: The gross profit margin is the ratio of gross profit to total revenue, expressed as a percentage. It is calculated by subtracting the cost of goods sold from total revenue and dividing the result by total revenue. It is a measure of the profitability of a company after subtracting the cost of goods sold.

Operating Income: Extractive Summary: Operating income research and development expenses increased due to investments in research and development for new and enhanced offerings and solutions. Sales and marketing expenses increased due to programs aimed at increasing revenue. General and administrative expenses increased due to charges related to the cancellation of corporate events and bad debt expense.
