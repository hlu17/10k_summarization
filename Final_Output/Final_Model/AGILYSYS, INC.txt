Business Overview: agilysys is a leader in hospitality software, providing innovative cloud-native SaaS and on-premise solutions for gaming, hotels, resorts, corporate foodservice, restaurants, universities, stadia, and healthcare. The company has a single reportable segment serving the global hospitality industry, operating across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and India with headquarters in Alpharetta, Georgia. agilysys' mission is to increase shareholder value with improved financial and operational performance and by growing the business through superior products and services. To achieve this, the company plans to invest a portion of its cash on hand in 23 existing software products, developing and marketing new software products, and expanding its customer base both vertically and geographically. The strategic plan focuses on creating shareholder value through profitable growth opportunities, tighter management of operating expenses, and concentrating investments on growth opportunities with the highest returns.

Results of Operations: Results of Operations: Total revenue decreased $23.6 million, or 14.7%, in fiscal 2021 compared to fiscal 2020. Products revenue decreased $17.5 million, or 39.6%, due to delayed customer purchasing decisions as the timing of the hospitality recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic remains unclear. Support, maintenance and subscription services revenue increased $4.9 million, or 5.8%, driven by continued growth in subscription-based revenue, which increased 15.5% in fiscal 2021 compared to fiscal 2020. Professional services revenue decreased $11.0 million, or 33.3%, due to delays in implementation services due to customer property restrictions, property closures and lower sales from delayed customer purchasing decisions as the timing of the hospitality recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic remains unclear. Gross profit increased $8.4 million, or 10.4%, in fiscal 2021 and total gross profit margin increased from 50.4% to 65.2%. Operating expenses, excluding the charges for legal settlements, impairments, severance and other charges, increased $16.8 million, or 18.5%, in fiscal 2021 compared with fiscal 2020. Product development increased $13.9 million, or 33.5%, during fiscal 2021 as compared to fiscal 2020 due to the continued expansion of the company's R&D teams and significant share-based compensation charges resulting from the accelerated vesting of stock-settled appreciation rights upon their market condition satisfaction in February 2021. Sales and marketing decreased $5.7 million, or 28.5%, in fiscal 2021 compared with fiscal 2020 due to reduced commission expense as a result of lower sales levels, significantly reduced travel, layoffs, temporary reductions in employee benefits, and the absence of in-person trade shows and conference activity. General and administrative increased $9.0 million, or 37.0%, in fiscal 2021 compared to fiscal 2020 due to significant share-based compensation charges resulting from the accelerated vesting of stock-settled appreciation rights. Impairments totaled $23.7 million in fiscal 2020. Severance and other charges increased $1.9 million due to an increase in non-restructuring severance activity during fiscal 2021 compared to fiscal 2020. Legal settlements consist of settlements of employment and other business-related matters.
