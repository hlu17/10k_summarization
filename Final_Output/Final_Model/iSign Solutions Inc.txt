Business Overview: The company is a leading supplier of DTM (Digital Transaction Management) software that enables paperless, secure, and cost-effective management and authentication of document-based transactions. Its solutions include electronic signatures, workflow management, and various options for biometric authentication, available across virtually all enterprise, desktop, and mobile environments. The company primarily serves the financial services and insurance industries, helping its customers significantly reduce expenses through electronic document and workflow processes.On May 6, 2020, the company received loan proceeds of approximately $123 under the Paycheck Protection Program (PPP). The company believes that its use of the loan proceeds meets the conditions for loan forgiveness, but there is no assurance that the company will not be found ineligible for forgiveness, in whole or in part.The company issued several warrants during 2020 to consultants and vendors for services and settlement of outstanding accounts payable. The total value of these warrants was determined using the Black-Scholes-Merton valuation model.The companys critical accounting policies involve judgments, assumptions, and estimates that affect the amounts reported in the financial statements.
