Business Overview: falconstor is a data protection leader that enables enterprises to modernize their data backup and archival operations across sites and public clouds. It delivers increased data security and providing the fastest recovery from a ransomware attack while driving down costs by up to 95 percent. The company has over an exabyte of data under management and is trusted by more than 600 enterprise customers and an ecosystem of managed service providers and resellers worldwide.  falconstor continues to focus on enterprise customers, managed service providers, and oem partners. revenue allocated to perpetual and term software licenses are recognized at a point in time upon electronic delivery of the download link and the license keys. product maintenance and support services are satisfied over time as they are stand-ready obligations throughout the support period.

Revenues: product revenue is comprised of both licenses for our software solutions and sales of the platforms on which the software is installed. products are sold through our oems, and through (i) value-added resellers, (ii) distributors and/or directly to end-users. these revenues are recognized when all the applicable criteria under accounting principles generally accepted in the united states are met. product revenue represented 48% and 41% of our total revenue for the years ended december 31, 2020 and 2019.
